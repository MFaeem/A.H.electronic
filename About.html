Our Story

Founded in 2010, Appliance Store has been serving customers with premium home
appliances for over a decade. We started as a small family business with a vision to
provide quality products at affordable prices.


Today, we have grown into a trusted retailer specializing in steel almirah,
refrigerators, washing machines, LED TVs, and other essential home appliances.
Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains unchanged.


We believe that every home deserves the best appliances, and we work tirelessly to
make that vision a reality for our customers.
